Who We Are

Pyr Hope Farm is a dog daycare, lodging and fostering facility that is situated on 13 beautiful, wooded and serene acres in Shelton, Washington. We’ve built this business around the concept that your pets’ safety and comfort are paramount. A stay at our facility will help your pet build social confidence while having a safe, fun and stimulating way to release energy.

Pry Hope Farm offers the following:

Overnight lodging

Doggie daycare

Limited occupancy = personalized care

900 sq ft indoor play area with padded floors

1000 sq ft outdoor play area with turf

Cedar Grove secure natural dog park

Nature walks

Seasonal stream

AND MUCH MORE!
