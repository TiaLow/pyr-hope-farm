Pyr Fostering & Resources

Join the Pyr Pack!

In 2011, we fell in love with the Great Pyrenees breed not only for their majestic beauty, but also their intelligence and loyalty. We adopted our first Pry Brody from Connections Pet Rescue (CPR) and soon after we became official Great Pyrenees foster parents. Their purpose is to rescue and rehabilitate Great Pyrenees and Great Pyrenees mixes that are abandoned and neglected, finding themselves in kill shelters.

We are looking for people that love animals and would be willing to open your home and foster an animal. CPR has puppies, adults, seniors and Pyrs with special needs that are looking for their forever home.

If you are interested in becoming a foster parent, talk to us or please visit Connections Pet Rescue to look at the foster process and requirements.

Thank you!
