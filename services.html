What We Do

We aren’t a mass boarding facility where you hear lots of dogs barking continually and others feeling anxious or scared. Instead, we purposefully created an environment that is relaxed and controlled with plenty of planned activities. The owners, Brent and Sophia Low, live on site and work hard to maintain safe and secure grounds with 24-hour monitoring and observation along with full back up power generatation for power outages.

Doggie Daycare

Pyr Hope Farm is adventurous! Our daycare program is for dogs that play well with others. Doggie daycare provides your pet with a fun and exciting environment for play and socialization. There are 3 outdoor play areas with turf and grass that are securely fenced and 1 indoor play area with padded floors, toys, dog pulley and Dog TV playing on the big screen. Daycare includes supervised playtime, human interaction, Dog TV, naptime and games. All pets are separated by size and play-style to promote a healthy play environment.

Overnight Lodging

Our state-of-the-art facility is Pyrfect for short-term and long-term boarding. In the lodging area your dog will enjoy soothing music, dimmed lighting and a temperature-controlled setting.

  • Standard accommodations include a private kennel outfitted with orthopedic bedding, breakfast, morning stretch, outdoor playtime, naptime and dinner.
  • Plush accommodations include a private kennel outfitted with pet cot and orthopedic bedding along with the standard accommodations above.

Vaccine Requirements

Veterinary proof of current DHPP (distemper/Parvo), Bordetella and Rabies. If we see active flea infestation, we will give your pet a flea bath for $15.00. We strongly recommend treating your pet with flea and tick prevention.

Bathing

Most boarding customers request our signature “Jail Break” or going home bath for their pets. This includes bath with hypoallergenic shampoo, pawdicure, blow out and brushing.
