Quick paragraph about variety of services, what sets you unique from other places?

Doggie Daycare

Pyr Hope Farm is adventurous! Our daycare program is for dogs that play well with others. Doggie daycare provides your pet with a fun and exciting environment for play and socialization. There is 1 indoor play area with padded floors, toys, dog pulley and 3 outdoor play areas. Daycare includes supervised playtime, human interaction, naptime and games. All pets are separated by size and play style to promote a healthy play.

4 hours $15
Day rate $25

Overnight Lodging

Our state-of-the-art facility is Pyrfect for short-term and long-term lodging. In the lodging area your dog will enjoy a 4 x 6 ft kennel, soothing sounds, dimmed lighting and a temperature-controlled setting.

Check-in and check-out time is noon daily.

Standard accomodation $35 / night
Plush accomodation $45 / night
Additional pet, same kennel $25 / night
  • Standard accommodations include a private kennel complete with orthopedic bedding, breakfast, morning stretch, outdoor playtime, naptime and dinner.
  • Plush accommodations include a private kennel complete with pet cot and orthopedic bedding along with the standard accommodations above.
